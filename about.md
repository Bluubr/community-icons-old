# 🎨 Community Icons

Tired of manually replacing texture files? **Community Icons** brings a community-driven marketplace directly into your Icon Kit! 

### ✨ Features
* **Live In-Game Browser**: Explore a growing library of community-made icons without ever leaving Geometry Dash.
* **Curated Content**: To ensure high quality, every submission is **reviewed by moderators** for approval before appearing in the shop.
* **Infinite Slots**: Powered by the **Texture Loader** framework—downloaded icons are added as brand-new slots rather than replacing your favorites.
* **One-Click Install**: Just hit "Download" and your new icon is instantly available for use.
* **Cross-Platform**: Fully compatible with Windows, Android and more!.

### 🛠 How to Use
1. Open your **Icon Kit** (Garage Layer).
2. Click the new **Workshop Button**.
3. Find an icon you love, then click **View** → **Download**.
4. Scroll to the end of your icons to find your new addition!

### 📦 Requirements
* **[Texture Loader](https://geode-sdk.org/mods/geode.texture-loader)** (Required)
* **[Node Ids](https://geode-sdk.org/mods/geode.node-ids)** (Required)
